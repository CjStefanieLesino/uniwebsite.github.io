Welcome to Dale University

We’re young and we’re curious. We’re constantly scanning what’s happening in the world and translating this into challenging, realistic and highly practical courses that prepare students for the future.

Our programs are developed in consultation with industry groups and we focus on our graduates' employability skills.

Practicums and networking events are integral to most of our courses and students can be offered paid internships as part of their studies.

At Dale University, we pride ourselves on being one of the Australia's most engaged univesities.

Offering technical and vocational certificates and diplomas as well as bachelor and post graduate degrees across a wide range of areas, we make education and training even more accessible and applicable to more people.

Why Study with Us?

A WEALTH OF CHOICES

From What you choose to study to how you choose to study, we've got an option to suit your needs. Choose from a broad variety of certifications and university courses from a wide range of study areas, with many offered across three terms, giving you the option to spped up your qualification or study when it suits you.
We're committed to delivering accessible learning options to everyone - no matter where you live or how you choose to study.

A WORLD-CLASS EDUCATION

From teaching and research, to knowledge transfer, international outlook, inclusiveness and distance education, Dale University has developed a strong reputation for delivering a high quality education experience and excellent graduate outcomes
We're proud to be delivering research with real world outcomes and are rated at above and well above world standards across various research categories.
When it comes to study support, graduate employment to graduate salary outcomes, Dale University also outperforms the majority of the nation's university
