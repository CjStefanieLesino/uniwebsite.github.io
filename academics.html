Types Of Scholars Bootstrap Example

Partial Scholarship

If you scored 90-94% in the entrance examination or part of the honorable mentions of the graduating batch, you can get a chance in this scholarship! This one covers half of the total tuition fee amount together with as well as 50% of the value of the other benefits that the full scholars enjoy. However, some institutions only pay for the basic tuition fee, so you should always double check the terms and exact coverage of the scholarship.

Athletic Scholarship

For those who are competing for Palarong Pambansa or outstanding school athletes, you can avail this scholarship! This can cover all or part of the tuition and may also include monthly stipend, since being a student-athlete is also full-time job. The scholarship varies depending on the terms of the scholarship because some schools will require you to maintain a certain grade while other schools require consistent excellent athletic performance on college.
