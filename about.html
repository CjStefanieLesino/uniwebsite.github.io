History of DALE UNIVERSITY

Though plans for a university in York first appeared in 2017 of January. Today, permission was finally granted for the Dale University to be built, marking the beginning of our journey.

Technology also moved fast, with "DaleWeb" becoming a major marketing tool, the introduction of a Virtual Learning Environment, and new ways of communicating with students and stakeholders through SMS, Facebook and on-campus electronic screens. We installed wi-fi across campus, video-conferencing and a range of online services.

The new Millennium saw a sea-change in the way that Philippines viewed higher education.

Unprecedented student numbers in universities and strong investment, was coupled with increasing regulation, compliance issues and requirements to demonstrate societal and economic impact.

Student debt, quality assurance, Freedom of Information and public scrutiny changed the way in which both academic departments and central support for the University’s mission operated.

Mission
